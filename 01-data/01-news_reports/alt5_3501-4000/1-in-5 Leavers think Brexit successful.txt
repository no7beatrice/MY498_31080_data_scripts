   1-in-5 Leavers think Brexit successful Western Daily Press June 23, 2023 Friday Edition 1, National Edition  Copyright 2023 Reach PLC All Rights Reserved Section: NEWS; Pg. 10 Length: 93 words Body   LESS than a fifth of Brexit voters believe it has been a success seven years on from the 2016 referendum, according to new polling. The findings, from an online survey of 4,005 UK adults from May 26 to June 2 and carried out by Public First for UK in a Changing Europe, show that 18% of Leave voters believe Brexit has gone "well" or "very well" - 30% believe it has "neither turned out well or badly", while 29% of Leave supporters think Brexit has had a negative economic impact on the UK. The poll found 72% of Leave voters want to stop talking about Brexit. Load-Date: June 23, 2023  End of Document

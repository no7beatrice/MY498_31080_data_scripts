   only 1 in 5 leavers say brexit goes well Daily Mirror June 22, 2023 Thursday Edition 1, Scotland  Copyright 2023 Reach PLC All Rights Reserved  Section: FEATURES; Pg. 10 Length: 141 words Byline: Ben glaze Body   ONLY 18% of Leave voters believe Brexit is a success. Tomorrow is the anniversary of the 2016 vote, when voters backed quitting the EU by 52% to 48%. Pollsters Public First for the UK in a Changing Europe thinktank found 18% of Leavers feel Brexit has gone "well" or "very well".UK in a Changing Europe director Prof Anand Menon said: "While very few people think Brexit is going well, a large number of Leavers also believe it is still too soon to make a definitive judgment. "Many Leave voters believe Brexit has not been analysis success because politicians have let them down. The danger is that this will lead to an erosion of faith in politics and politicians." But Rishi Sunak insisted yesterday: "We have actually grown faster than France and Italy since we left the Single Market." To read the full analysis, go to www.mirror.co.uk.
